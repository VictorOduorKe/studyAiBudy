How It Works

This guide will help you navigate the website and get started quickly.

  • 1. Create an Account

    Go to the Sign Up page (link located at the top left of the website). After successfully creating an account, you will be redirected to the Login page.

  • 2. Log In

    If you are not redirected automatically, click the Sign In link. Enter the email and password you used during registration to log in.

  • 3. Main Dashboard

    Once logged in, you’ll see the main dashboard with tabs: Dashboard, Generate Plan, Logout, and Settings. On the dashboard:

    • Enter a subject name (e.g., Mathematics) in the input box, choose the right education stage eg(form two) and click Add.
    • If successful, you’ll see a confirmation message and the new subject will appear below the form.
    • You can delete a subject using the red Delete button.
      ⚠️ Note: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

  • 4. Generate a Study Plan

    Navigate to the Generate Plan tab. Your added subjects will appear with a green Generate button. Click it and wait for the AI model to create your plan.

    Once ready, the button will change to black with the label View Plan. Click it to open your study plan.

    The study plan page has two sections:

    • Roadmap – a 7-week structured study plan with topics and goals.
    • Quizzes – practice quizzes to test your understanding.

    You can attempt quizzes multiple times, but only your first attempt will be saved.

    🎉 Congratulations on reaching this step and getting your personalized study plan!
